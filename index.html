3. Information We Collect

SMS Notification Access

The App uses Android's Notification Listener Service to detect incoming M-Pesa transaction notifications from your default SMS messaging app.

The App does NOT have permission to read your SMS inbox directly. It only processes notifications that are posted by known SMS apps and contain both "M-PESA" and "Confirmed" keywords — confirming they are genuine M-Pesa transaction notifications.

Notifications from all other apps are discarded immediately without any processing or storage.

The App may extract from M-Pesa notifications:

  • Transaction date and time
  • Transaction amount
  • Transaction type
  • Merchant or recipient name
  • Transaction reference number
  • Account balance information contained in M-Pesa SMS messages

No other SMS or notification content is accessed or stored.

Financial Information

The App may process information that you manually enter, including:

  • Income
  • Expenses
  • Savings goals
  • Budget categories
  • Investment preferences
  • Financial notes

Device Information

The App may collect limited technical information necessary for normal operation, including:

  • Android version
  • Device model
  • Application version

No device identifiers are collected for advertising purposes.

6. Data Storage

On-Device Storage (Financial Data)

All financial data — including your M-Pesa transactions, manual entries, savings goals, investments, and financial records — is stored exclusively on your device.

This data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption (SQLCipher) and never transmitted to our servers or any third party. We have no server. We cannot access your financial data.

Firebase Analytics (Usage Data Only) With your explicit consent during onboarding, the App sends anonymous usage behavior to Google Firebase Analytics. This includes which screens you visit and which features you use.

The following is NEVER sent to Firebase or any server:

  • Transaction amounts or balances
  • M-Pesa transaction codes
  • Counterparty names or phone numbers
  • Your savings goals or investment values
  • Any financial figures whatsoever

You can withdraw analytics consent at any time in Settings → Profile → toggle off Analytics.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Where you have consented to analytics during onboarding, anonymous usage behavior (screen views, feature usage — never financial data) is shared with Google Firebase Analytics to help us improve the App.

Financial data is never shared with any party under any circumstances.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no electronic storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8A. Encryption and Security

Financial Database Encryption

All your financial data is stored in an AES-256 encrypted database (SQLCipher) on your device. Even if someone accesses your device storage directly, they cannot read your financial data without the encryption key, which is unique to your device and secured by Android Keystore.

App Lock

The App requires a 4-digit PIN or biometric authentication every time it is opened, providing an additional layer of protection for your financial information.

12. Permissions Used

Notification Listener Service

Used solely to detect M-Pesa transaction notifications from your default SMS messaging app. This is NOT the same as SMS read permission — the App cannot access your SMS inbox. Only M-Pesa transaction notifications are processed.

Biometric Permission

Used optionally to unlock the App using your fingerprint or face. Biometric data never leaves your device and is managed entirely by Android's secure biometric system.

Internet Permission

Required solely to communicate with Google Firebase Analytics (with your consent) and to open partner referral links in your browser when you tap them.

13. International Data Transfers

Where you have consented to Firebase Analytics, anonymous usage data (never financial data) may be processed on Google's infrastructure internationally in accordance with Google's privacy practices and Standard Contractual Clauses under GDPR.
