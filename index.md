## 3. Information We Collect

Depending on the permissions you grant, the App may process the following information:

### SMS Messages

The App requests permission to read SMS messages only to identify and analyze M-Pesa transaction messages for financial management purposes.

The App may extract information such as:

* Transaction date and time
* Transaction amount
* Transaction type
* Merchant or recipient name
* Transaction reference number
* Account balance information contained in M-Pesa SMS messages

The App does **not** access SMS messages unrelated to financial transaction analysis.

### Financial Information

The App may process information that you manually enter, including:

* Income
* Expenses
* Savings goals
* Budget categories
* Investment preferences
* Financial notes

### Device Information

The App may collect limited technical information necessary for normal operation, including:

* Android version
* Device model
* Application version

No device identifiers are collected for advertising purposes.
